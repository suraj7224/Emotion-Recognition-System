WHAT IS EMOTION RECOGNITION?

Emotion Recognition is one of the crucial non-verbal means by which the communication occurs, it helps identify mood and state of a person. Human emotions can be classified as fear, contempt, disgust, anger, surprise, happy, neutral, etc. These emotions are very subtle and can vary from person to person. Human computer interaction have become a major part of our lives and although still lack the basic means of recognizing and responding to non-verbal cues of attitudes, emotions and mental state.
